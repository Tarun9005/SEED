About S.E.E.D

This society was established in year 2015

This society aims at exploring and implementing new ideas and hence developing new skills.

A team of around 30 members which works together in developing new ideas and technology in fields related to IoT, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence etc.

Androcar

Main Components

It consists of various components like Arduino-Uno, Bluetooth Module(HC-06), Motor Driving IC(L298x) and an app to control the car wirelessly.

Androcar Workshop

This event is based on building a car being controlled by an android smartphone,with the help of bluetooth.It involves no android coding and is very educative.

Zealicon Prizes

A competition is held in Zealicon on a track which is full of hurdles and prizes are awarded to 1st and 2nd postion holders.

R.C Plane

Main Components

It consists of various components like Transmiiter-Receiver, Battery, Electronic Speed Controllers(E.S.C), Servo Motors a sheet of Coroplast to make the body of the plane.

R.C Plane Workshop

This event is based on building a plane being controlled by a remote,with the help of a transmitter and a receiver.It involves use of aerodynamics principles and simple electronics to make a remote controlled plane.

Zealicon Prizes

A competition is held in Zealicon with various points divided on landing and taking off the plane and prizes are awarded to 1st and 2nd postion holders.

Home Automation

Home automation is building automation for a home, called a smart home.In this event paricipants are provided with circuit diagrams of various electronic components and how to connect them and get desired output.They are required to complete the connections in minimum time.

Criminal Minds

It is an event in which clues are placed all over the campus and participants have to search for around 10 clues.Each clue leads to another and finally the team finishing with minimum time wins the event.Participants can participate with a team of 4 individuals.

Minute to Win

This is an event in which participants are required to complete certain tasks within a minute.There are various task like putting maximum number of tennis balls in a cup in less than a minute etc. The participant who completes all the task in minimum time is declared as winner.
